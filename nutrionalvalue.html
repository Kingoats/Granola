1. Calories in Granola

Granola cereal contains high calories and is a vibrant source of protein, fiber, vitamins, and minerals for the body. Thanks to its diverse composition, Granola contains many good minerals for the body such as iron, magnesium, zinc, copper, selenium, B vitamins, and vitamin E...

However, there are many different types of Granola. Depending on how each person/manufacturer needs to follow their dietary needs, lose weight, gain weight, or for the gymer … will have different nutritional values.

Basically, Granola will contain the following nutritional ingredients (figures in 29 grams of Granola = 140 calories)

Fat: 9g


Carbs: 14g


Fiber: 3g


Protein: 3g


Sugar: 4g

Granola at King Outs up to 70% - 100% of nutritional seeds and dried fruits, oats only account for 0% - 30%. Thus ensuring a full supply of essential nutrients for the body, especially do not use sugar but only using pure honey and dried fruit to create natural sweetness, extremely suitable for those who want to eat and lose weight.

2. Weight loss menu with Granola

2.1. Breakfasts

You should eat granola every morning to completely replace the usual high-calorie and fatty foods such as vermicelli, and pho... Every morning you should eat about 2 tablespoons of granola (equivalent to no more than 50 grams).

Combine with 1 jar of yogurt or fresh milk without sugar. In addition, you can add about 1 teaspoon of chia seeds to help you increase the feeling of fullness for a long time, reducing the feeling of hunger.

Regarding fruit, you can add some fruits such as banana (1 fruit), apple (1/2 fruit), strawberry (4 fruit), blueberry (5 fruit)… Fresh fruit will help with the breakfast dish more variety, and will not be boring.

2.2. Snacks

In addition to eating in the morning, you can also eat granola for snacks – For example, a snack before going to the gym…also very good for weight loss.

At these meals, you can eat granola directly as a healthy snack (for super crunchy granola), or eat it with some fruits to reduce boredom.

The menu above is a very simple and effective menu for your granola weight loss process. Try applying the above menu every morning and feel a positive change in the body.
